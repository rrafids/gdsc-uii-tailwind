Student Clubs

Universitas Islam Indonesia x UPN-V Yogyakarta

GDSC UII are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC UII, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
